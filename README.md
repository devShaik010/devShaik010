## About Me

I am an engineer who enjoys working across the full lifecycle of a system, from design and development to deployment, scaling, operations, and security.

> "Good engineering is not just about making things work. It is about making them reliable when it matters."

I have a strong foundation in both software development and DevOps engineering. I understand how applications are built, how infrastructure supports them, and how systems behave once they are running in real environments.

My current role is focused on DevOps and operations. I work with my team on infrastructure, deployments, automation, monitoring, reliability, and delivery timelines. The goal is simple: keep systems stable, scalable, secure, and ready for production.

Beyond engineering, I mentor 250+ learners through AWS Cloud Club and TSIG by conducting workshops, hands-on labs, and technical sessions. Teaching helps me stay sharp, and sharing knowledge is one of the best ways to grow.
